Senior Java/J2EE Developer


  • Melbourne CBD Location

  • Blue Chip Client

  • Enterprise level Java delivery experience

So what made you click on this advert?

Are you not happy in your current position?

Feeling that you are being held back & not having the chance to grow and experience different projects & technologies?

Missing that excitement when you first started in your development career?

Well, my ‘exclusive client’ has a “Once in a career opportunity” for you to get your career back on track. You will be joining "the elite dev team" in the company’s progressive coding group.

Joining an organisation such as this will see you finally take control of your career and allow you the opportunity to step up. Whether in the future you would like an opportunity to take on the responsibility of leading or training a team, or you would like to experience a different area of IT, in a company of this size you don’t have to leave to progress your career!

This is a fantastic opportunity to work with cutting edge technology within an enterprise level organisation among a team of senior, high quality developers. You will be involved in end to end development using open source technologies across various projects.

My client is looking for someone who is hungry and excited by new technologies. An individual who is quality oriented and passionate about 'clean code'. In order to be considered for this role you must be a developer who believes in Test-driven Development (TDD), while also considering Behaviour-driven Development (BDD).

This team is recognised as the most progressive and exciting team to work in across the organisation. They are looking for a like minded individual; someone who is willing to really get involved and push boundaries in technology. Joining this team of developers will give you the chance to shine and finally gain the recognition you deserve.

Experience is needed with the following:

  • Java/J2EE design & development experience
  • Spring, Hibernate, AngularJS, RESTful Web Services, GIT
  • Strong delivery & quality focus
  • Experience growing an application and putting it into production

This is a great opportunity for the right person to reach the top in a major organisation and realise their true potential. You will be passionate about making an impact on projects and business outcomes to the highest level.

If this sounds like you then hit the apply button now or call Brona on (03) 8641 6806 for a confidential chat
